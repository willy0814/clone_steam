What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We're thrilled to announce our decision to release our upcoming game in Early Access on Steam! We believe that Early Access offers an incredible opportunity for both our development team and the gaming community. By choosing Early Access, we're inviting players to join us on an exciting journey of creation and refinement. It allows us to collaborate directly with our player base, gather valuable feedback, and make real-time improvements based on your input. This process ensures that we can create the best possible gaming experience together.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Our commitment to delivering a polished gaming experience is paramount, and we anticipate that our Early Access phase will last approximately six months to a year. During this time, we'll be diligently collecting feedback, addressing issues, and adding exciting new features based on player suggestions and our own vision.”
